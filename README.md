# Gravitational Wave Polarization Modes

This repository contains Mathematica notebooks developed during my M.Sc. research in gravitational physics.

The project includes:

* Symbolic derivations of the Ricci scalar and metric perturbation equations up to zeroth and first order.
* Computation of gravitational-wave polarization modes through the geodesic deviation equation at first order in metric perturbations.
* Simulations and visualizations of gravitational-wave polarization patterns, including plus, cross, breathing, longitudinal, mixed, and circular modes.

These notebooks were developed as part of my research on alternative gravitational-wave polarizations in modified theories of gravity.
